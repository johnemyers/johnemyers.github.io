What is Agile Vibe?

Humans decide, AI suggests

Agile Vibe blends human collaboration with a co‑pilot that offers suggestions, never mandates. Insight nudges and contextual code help reduce toil—while choice stays with the team.

Dual visibility: tech + feelings

Dashboards combine performance signals (latency, errors) and team safety signals (focus‑flow, psychological safety) so you can spot issues in code and culture.

Safe velocity

Feature flags, green‑blue deploys, and intention triggers let you ship often without betting the vibe. Observability closes the loop and makes learning continuous.

Core Principles

Ambient Consent

Collaboration runs on subtle invitations—pairing asks, not assigns; cues that keep flow respectful like a smooth dance floor.

Emotional Refactoring

Conflict is logged and debugged with patience, just like flaky tests. Feelings are first‑class citizens in the process.

Chosen‑Family Dependency Injection

Loose coupling in systems, intentional attachment in teams. Mentorship is opt‑in, multi‑directional, and kind.

Governance Without Gremlins

The Vibe‑Limitations Manifesto

  1. Humans decide, co‑pilot suggests
  2. Consent‑first pairing
  3. Transparent metrics
  4. Weekly Analog Hour
  5. Learning budget

Practical Guardrails

  • AI suggestions require human “yes.”
  • Pair invites, never auto‑assignments.
  • Sentiment scrubs names; anonymize by default.
  • Dual‑key for AI‑authored commits.
  • Right‑to‑be‑forgotten for crush‑data and embeddings.

Adopt the Vibe in 3 Steps

1) Start with the ritual

Run a weekly Analog Hour. Disable AI assistance and ship a tiny feature together—keyboards, tests, and conversation only.

2) Wire the loop

Add a “vibe” panel to your existing dashboard. Pair latency, error rate, and deployment health with a short focus‑flow poll.

3) Guardrail the bots

Enable the governance plugin: cap auto‑fixes, block auto‑reassign, and enforce Analog Hour. Suggestions are welcome; sovereignty stays human.
